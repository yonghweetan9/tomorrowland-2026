# Tomorrowland 2026 Home-Screen Icon — Design Brief & Prompts

## This year's theme (the source material)

Tomorrowland 2026's global theme is **Consciencia** (Portuguese for *conscience*). It's built on the idea that existence rests on **six primal emotions**: **Wonder, Love, Anger, Joy, Desire, Sadness** — each one a "faction" with its own colour and visual language. The mainstage is expected to centre on a **giant sculpted human face / mask** in aged marble or cracked stone, threaded with glowing **neural pathways / memory streams**, mixing classical architecture with Tomorrowland's signature steampunk-fantasy.

Emotion → colour palette I'm using (so the icon stays on-theme):

- Wonder → deep indigo / violet
- Love → rose / crimson
- Anger → ember orange-red
- Joy → warm gold
- Desire → magenta
- Sadness → cyan-blue

The festival's own brand red is roughly `#E41F17`.

## How I'm thinking about the design (the constraints that matter)

A phone home-screen icon is the hardest format in graphic design because it's tiny. The thinking behind every prompt below:

- **One focal element, readable at ~60px.** When this sits on your home screen it's the size of a fingernail. No fine text, no thin lines, no busy scene — just one bold silhouette that reads instantly.
- **Full-bleed square, no rounded corners of your own.** iOS automatically masks the icon into a rounded square (a "squircle"). So the art must fill the whole square edge-to-edge; if you add your own rounded corners you get an ugly double-border.
- **High contrast against any wallpaper.** A rich, dark, saturated background so it pops whether your wallpaper is light or dark — not a white/pale icon that disappears.
- **Glow over detail.** Festival energy reads better as light, gradient and bloom than as tiny intricate ornament that turns to mush when shrunk.
- **On-theme, not literal.** It should *feel* like Consciencia (emotion, marble, neon, night) rather than cram in a logo, a date and a lineup.

I've made the three options genuinely different so you have a real choice: one **iconic** (the face), one **abstract/geometric** (the six emotions), one **atmospheric** (the party). Each prompt is self-contained — paste the whole block into Claude Design as-is.

## OPTION 1 — "The Consciencia Mask" (iconic / premium)

**Why this one:** Leans on the festival's actual 2026 centrepiece — the sculpted face. A single bold marble mask makes an unmistakable, high-end icon that looks like an official app.

> Design a square mobile app icon, full-bleed with no rounded corners (it will be auto-masked). Subject: a single frontal sculpted human face like a classical marble mask, serene and symmetrical, filling most of the frame. The marble is cracked, and through the cracks glow luminous neural pathways and circuitry in six emotion colours — indigo, rose, ember-orange, gold, magenta and cyan — like light leaking from inside. Deep near-black background with a subtle radial glow behind the face. Cinematic, premium, mysterious, festival-fantasy mood. Strong central silhouette that stays legible at very small sizes. Rich saturated colour, soft volumetric bloom, no text, no logos, no thin fragile details. Style: polished 3D render, dramatic rim lighting.

---

## OPTION 2 — "Six Emotions Emblem" (abstract / geometric)

**Why this one:** The cleanest, most logo-like and most timeless. A radial emblem split into six glowing segments literally *is* the Consciencia concept (six emotions, one consciousness). Scales beautifully and reads instantly.

> Design a square mobile app icon, full-bleed with no rounded corners (it will be auto-masked). Subject: a symmetrical circular emblem / mandala divided into six radiating segments, each a different glowing emotion colour — indigo (wonder), rose (love), ember-orange (anger), gold (joy), magenta (desire), cyan (sadness) — meeting at a single bright core at the centre, suggesting many emotions becoming one consciousness. Geometric, sacred-geometry feel, faint neural filigree lines connecting the segments. Deep charcoal-black background so the colours glow like neon. Bold, balanced, instantly legible at small sizes. Smooth gradients, soft outer glow, no text, no letters, no thin breakable lines. Style: modern luminous vector emblem with subtle depth.

---

## OPTION 3 — "Festival Night" (atmospheric / party)

**Why this one:** The most energetic and the most obviously "a party". Captures the rave feeling — night, stage, fireworks, crowd — distilled into one warm glowing silhouette. Pick this if you want fun over formal.

> Design a square mobile app icon, full-bleed with no rounded corners (it will be auto-masked). Subject: a stylised fantasy festival mainstage at night, an ornate curved silhouette crowned by a single sculpted face, with a luminous tree of life or butterfly motif glowing at its centre and bursts of fireworks above. A magenta-to-indigo night-sky gradient, gold and ember stage lights, a faint silhouette of raised hands along the bottom edge. Dreamy, euphoric, celebratory mood. One clear glowing focal shape that reads at small sizes. Saturated colour, strong glow and bloom, no text, no logos, no tiny details. Style: cinematic poster illustration with deep depth and light.
